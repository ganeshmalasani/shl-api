Assessment Fact Sheet 

Overview 

The Data Entry Numeric Split Screen test measures the candidate’s speed and accuracy at typing 
numbers in a form using the numeric keypad or the number keys at the top of the keyboard. The text 
original information is displayed directly above the area in which the candidate responds. The 
information includes business-related numbers such as office number, item number, model number, 
and billing code. Candidates will be scored on speed and accuracy.
