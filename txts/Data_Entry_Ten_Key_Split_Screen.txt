Overview 

The Data Entry Ten Key Split Screen test measures the candidate’s ability to enter numbers using the 
numeric keypad on the keyboard (the number keys at the top of the keyboard are disabled). The text 
original information is displayed to the left of the area in which the candidate responds. The information 
includes positive and negative numbers. Candidates will be scored on speed and accuracy.
