Description:
SHL Live Video Interview is a real-time video interview tool. It allows recruiters and line managers to remotely engage with candidates and reach hiring decisions faster. 
Delight shortlisted candidates with a face2face digital interview that goes way beyond a conference call. Interact with whiteboards, instant file-sharing, draw from an expertly-curated question bank and provide a remote end-to-end interview experience that increases your chance of conversion.
