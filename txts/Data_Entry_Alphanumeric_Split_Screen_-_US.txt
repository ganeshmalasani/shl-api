Data Entry Alphanumeric Split Screen 

Assessment Fact Sheet 

Overview 

Details 

The Data Entry Alphanumeric Split Screen test assesses a candidate’s ability to enter alphabetical and 
numerical information into a table in a word processing document using a keyboard. The text original 
information is displayed directly above the area in which the candidate responds. The information 
includes business-related text and numbers such as office number, company name, address, mail code, 
item number, model number, and billing code. Candidates will be scored on speed and accuracy.
