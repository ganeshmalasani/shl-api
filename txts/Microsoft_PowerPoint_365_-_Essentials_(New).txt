Overview

The Microsoft PowerPoint 365 – Essentials simulation evaluates experience in creating, and saving,
presentations, adding and arranging multimedia elements, applying animations, formatting slides
and presentation content, modifying the presentation layout, and reviewing and delivering
presentations. The Essentials version consists of the 20 most important and frequently performed
tasks.
